About College

S. M. B. Torgalkar Junior College , Ratnagiri

In the year 2016-17, considering the increasing number of post-10th students in Ratnagiri district, Ratnagiri Education Society on 14th July 2016 R. B. Shirke Junior College started. This college was inaugurated by the then working president Adv. Patne Sir as well as Adv. Vijay Sakhalkar Sir, Acting Mrs. Prachi Joshi, Governing Body Member Shri. It was done by Manoj Patankar Sir.

It's Recently named as Shirmaan Mangesh Bapuji Torgalkar Junior College on 10th February 2024.

About S.M.B.T.

Shreeman Mangesh Bapuji Torgalkar Junior College was established under the Ratnagiri Education Society in 2016. The college consists of 2 major Faculty namely — Science and Commerce . At our college, we prioritize student comfort and aim to create a supportive atmosphere that fosters focused study and goal attainment. Beyond academic excellence, we are committed to instilling values of integrity, responsibility, and social awareness in our students.

Our mission is to empower students to excel academically while also preparing them to become compassionate and responsible members of society. Through our comprehensive approach to education, we strive to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their chosen fields and contribute positively to their communities.

2016

In response to the escalating number of post-10th students in Ratnagiri district during the academic year 2016-17, the Ratnagiri Education Society took proactive measures by establishing R. B. Shirke Junior College on July 14, 2016.

It was recently renamed as Shreeman Mangesh Bapuji Torgalkar Junior College on 10th Feb, 2024.

Glimpse of Our College

Labs

Chemistry Lab

Chemicals and other equipment material required for the subject of Chemistry are well classified in the laboratory. Also arrangement of the burner and gas-cylinder is in good condition. Chemistry lab is designed to allow students experiment safely.

Biology Lab

The Biology lab is equipped with living specimens for students to study. The laboratory is designed for the use of microscopes.The Lab has cupboards to keep materials. The Biology lab placed in such a place where sufficient light source is available.

Physics Lab

There is a separate laboratory arrangement for Physics. Students are encouraged to do experimental studies due to availability of electrical equipments, power supply facilities & other experimental materials required for the subject in sufficient quantity.

IT Lab

Creating an optimal learning environment the Information Technology Lab, equipped with 40 Windows 10 computers, that is planned effectively .Which ensure seamless operations of the practicals going to be conducted and helps in effective teaching.
